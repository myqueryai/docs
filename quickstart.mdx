## Welcome to MyQuery Docs

Create, customize, and publish your documentation instantly — no local setup required.

### Step 1: Access Your Docs Dashboard

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="dashboard" title="Open your Documentation Dashboard">
    Log in to your [MyQuery dashboard](https://dashboard.myquery.ai) to access all your documentation projects.
    <br /><br />
    From the dashboard, you can:
    <ul>
      <li>Create new documentation pages</li>
      <li>Edit or update existing docs using our visual editor</li>
      <li>Organize content with folders and sections</li>
      <li>Invite team members to collaborate</li>
    </ul>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 2: Customize Your Docs

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="palette" title="Brand your documentation site">
    From the dashboard settings:
    <ol>
      <li>Go to <b>Site Settings</b></li>
      <li>Update your site name and upload your logo</li>
      <li>Choose your primary and secondary colors to match your brand</li>
      <li>Save changes to see your branded docs instantly</li>
    </ol>
    <Tip>All customization is live — your docs update instantly for your users.</Tip>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 3: Go Live

<Accordion icon="rocket" title="Publish and share">
  - All documentation updates are published instantly to your public (or private) docs site.
  - Share the link with your team or customers — no deployment or technical setup needed.
</Accordion>
